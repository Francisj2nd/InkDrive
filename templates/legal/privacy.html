Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 2025

Welcome to InkDrive. We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data in an open and transparent manner. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our AI-powered content generation service ("Service").

1. Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us, information that is generated automatically when you use our Service, and information from third-party sources.

1.1. Information You Provide

  • Account Information: When you register for an account, we collect your name, email address, and password. If you register using a third-party service like Google, we collect the information provided by that service, such as your name, email, and profile picture.
  • Content You Create: We collect the inputs you provide to our AI models (e.g., topics, keywords, instructions) and the content generated by the Service (e.g., articles, social media posts, emails). This includes your chat history and refinement requests.
  • Communications: If you contact us directly for support or other inquiries, we may receive additional information about you, such as the contents of your message.

1.2. Information We Collect Automatically

  • Usage Data: We automatically collect information about your interactions with our Service. This includes the number of articles generated, words generated, documents downloaded, and features used. This helps us monitor and enforce usage quotas.
  • Device and Browser Information: We collect standard log data, including your IP address, browser type, operating system, and the dates and times of your access.
  • Cookies: We use cookies to operate and administer our Service, and to improve your experience. For more information, see our "Cookies and Tracking" section below.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To Provide and Maintain the Service: To operate our platform, process your requests, generate content, and manage your account.
  • To Improve and Personalize the Service: To understand how our users interact with the Service, develop new features, and personalize your experience. We may use anonymized and aggregated data to train our AI models.
  • For Security and Fraud Prevention: To protect the security of our Service, prevent fraud, and enforce our Terms of Service.
  • To Communicate with You: To send you service-related announcements, updates, and marketing communications (from which you can opt out).
  • To Comply with Legal Obligations: To meet our legal requirements and respond to lawful requests from public authorities.

3. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information in the following limited circumstances:

  • With Service Providers: We work with third-party service providers to help us operate our Service (e.g., hosting, data storage, AI model providers). These providers are contractually obligated to protect your data and are prohibited from using it for any other purpose.
  • With AI Model Providers (Google AI): The inputs you provide to our AI tools are sent to Google's AI models to generate content. We do not allow third parties to use your data to train their models.
  • For Legal Reasons: We may disclose your information if required by law, subpoena, or other legal process, or if we have a good faith belief that disclosure is necessary to protect our rights, your safety, or the safety of others.
  • With Your Consent: We may share your information with other third parties with your explicit consent.

4. Data Security

We implement robust security measures to protect your information, including:

  • Encryption: We use TLS to encrypt data in transit and encrypt sensitive data at rest.
  • Access Controls: We limit access to your personal information to employees and contractors who have a business need to know.
  • Secure Infrastructure: We use reputable cloud providers that adhere to high security standards.

While we take these precautions, no security system is impenetrable, and we cannot guarantee the absolute security of your data.

5. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access and Correction: You can access and update your account information through your profile settings.
  • Data Portability: You can download the articles you've created at any time.
  • Deletion: You can delete your account at any time from your profile settings. This will permanently delete your account and all associated data within 30 days.
  • Opt-out of Communications: You can opt out of receiving promotional emails from us by using the unsubscribe link within each email.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you with the Service. We may also retain information to comply with our legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements.

7. Children's Privacy

Our Service is not directed to children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will notify you by posting the new policy on this page and, where appropriate, by other means such as email.
