Frequently Asked Questions

How does the AI content generation work? expand_more

InkDrive uses state-of-the-art AI models from Google to generate content. When you provide a prompt in one of our studios, we send that information to the AI, which then creates a unique piece of content based on your instructions. You can then refine this content using our editing tools.

What are the different "Studios" for? expand_more

Each studio is a specialized workspace designed for a specific type of content. For example:

  • Article Studio: For long-form content like blog posts and essays.
  • Social Studio: For creating posts for platforms like Twitter and LinkedIn.
  • SEO Studio: For developing keyword strategies and auditing on-page SEO.
  • E-commerce Studio: For writing product descriptions and promotional campaigns.

Each studio has unique inputs and tools tailored to its purpose.

What are the limits of the free plan? expand_more

Our free plan is designed to let you experience the power of InkDrive. It includes a monthly quota for word generation and document downloads. You can view your current usage and limits on your dashboard. Quotas reset at the beginning of each month.

Who owns the content I create? expand_more

You do. As stated in our Terms of Service, you retain full ownership of the content you generate using InkDrive. You are free to use it for personal or commercial purposes, provided it adheres to our acceptable use policy.

Is the generated content unique and plagiarism-free? expand_more

The AI generates new content for each request, so the output is original. However, because AI models are trained on vast amounts of text from the internet, it is possible for the output to be similar to existing content. We always recommend reviewing and editing the generated content, and using a plagiarism checker if you have concerns.

How can I delete my account and data? expand_more

You can delete your account at any time from your profile settings page. Please be aware that this action is irreversible and will permanently delete your account and all of your created content from our systems.
