Frequently Asked Questions

How does InkDrive's AI article generation work? expand_more

InkDrive uses advanced AI models to generate comprehensive, SEO-optimized articles based on your topic input. Our AI analyzes your prompt and creates structured content with headings, paragraphs, and relevant image placeholders. You can then refine the content using natural language instructions.

What are the usage limits for free accounts? expand_more

Free accounts include 30,000 words per month, 10 document downloads per month, and 5 refinements per article. Usage limits reset monthly. Guest users get 1 refinement per article without an account.

Can I publish and share my articles? expand_more

Yes! You can publish your articles to make them publicly accessible via a shareable link. Published articles appear in our social proof sections and can be viewed by anyone with the link. You maintain full control over publishing and can unpublish at any time.

How do I download my articles? expand_more

You can download any article as a DOCX file directly from the article interface or your dashboard. The downloaded file includes all text content, images, and formatting. Downloads count toward your monthly quota.

Is my data secure and private? expand_more

Absolutely. We use industry-standard encryption and security measures to protect your data. Your articles and personal information are stored securely, and we never share your content without permission. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

Can I delete my account and data? expand_more

Yes, you can delete your account at any time from your profile settings. This will permanently remove all your articles, chat history, and personal data from our systems within 30 days.
